OUR MISSION


Our mission is to provide our customers with a safe and dependable transportation service with an emphasis on customer service and value. We will do this by observing time-tested traditional values.

OUR VALUES


Through our Value statement we commit –

To provide a community-oriented working environment with open and interactive communication.

To treat all our team members and customers with dignity and respect.

To operate as individuals with impeccable honesty, integrity and trustworthiness.

To conduct our business in an ethical and moral fashion.

To operate as individuals and as a company in a manner that creates a safe environment for EVERYONE – ALL THE TIME.

To train and empower our employees to be the best in the industry.

To work as a team throughout our organization.

To demonstrate operational excellence by preserving and caring for our clients’ well-being and the community that we serve.

To uphold professionalism and organization as best practices of doing business.

To take responsibility for our actions.

To achieve sufficient earnings to support long-term growth, employee satisfaction and job security for everyone.

To function in a charitable, civic and community-minded manner.

OUR VISION


At Zip Ride, our people are our greatest competitive advantage. By focusing on our values we will realize our Vision of making Zip Ride a world class industry leader in Transportation. The world we want to see materialize is one in which the playing field is leveled for those with visual or sensory impairment, as well as the Elderly.

Unfortunately, the aforementioned persons are further penalized if they live outside of a metropolitan area, like Nashville, with many resources to assist. Zip Ride seeks to provide an additional safety net for our customers who live in the surrounding counties so that they can continue to contribute to society without a high percentage of their wages being overwhelmingly funneled to their transportation.

Instead, we endeavor to allow them more financial flexibility and empowerment.

We will always strive to improve our processes and procedures and take advantage of emerging technologies that will continue to help our customers and employees.

ABOUT US

Zip Ride, LLC is committed to serving the ADA community. It’s CEO, Larmonz “Monzie” McDaniel, a Nashville native, earned a Bachelor of Science degree from The University of Tennessee and his Master’s in Business Administration from Trevecca Nazarene University.

Monzie is also visually impaired. After being dissatisfied with services or lack thereof provided by other car share companies, he was inspired to birth Zip Ride, LLC.

Zip Ride was created as a customer-driven company focused on giving the premier experience one needs.

Zip Ride is fully equipped to communicate with deaf and hard-of hearing population. Minimizing the challenges that many face when using transportation companies.

Our goal is to assist each client and offer the best service possible.
